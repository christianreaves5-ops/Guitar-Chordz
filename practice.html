Practice Plan

10 minutes a day, no drama.

10-Minute Routine

  1. 1
    Tune your guitar 1 min
  2. 2
    Warm up fingers 2 min
  3. 3
    Practice chord shapes (G, C, D, Em, Am) 3 min
  4. 4
    Switching drills (see right panel) 3 min
  5. 5
    Play a simple song or pattern 1 min

Switching Drills

  • G ↔ C
  • C ↔ D
  • D ↔ Em
  • Em ↔ Am
  • Am ↔ C
  • G ↔ D
  • C ↔ Em
