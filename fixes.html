Beginner Problems & Fixes

If it sounds bad, it’s usually one of these.

◎

Buzzing Strings

Common Cause

Not pressing close enough to the fret wire, or muting another string.

Quick Fix

Move your finger closer to the fret (not on it). Curl your fingertip and press only as hard as needed.

△

Finger Pain

Common Cause

New fingertips + pressing too hard + long sessions without breaks.

Quick Fix

Practice in short sessions (5–10 minutes). Use light pressure. Build calluses gradually.

Safety Note: Stop if you feel sharp pain.
◎

Slow Switching

Common Cause

Lifting fingers too far away from the strings and not using an anchor finger.

Quick Fix

Keep fingers close to strings. Move as a “shape.” Practice slowly and increase speed later.
